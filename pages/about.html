FUTURA

Exhibitions are art's heartbeat

FUTURA is the leading independent gallery guide for an engaged audience of collectors, curators, art professionals and art lovers.
Our gallery provide an accurate and curated resource, making world-class exhibitions accessible to a wide international audience. FUTURA collects, preserves, and interprets modern and contemporary art, and explores ideas across cultures through dynamic curatorial and educational initiatives and collaborations.
